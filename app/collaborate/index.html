You're invited

LIL is a hallway connecting three rooms of party guests - law folks, tech folks, and library folks. It's a fantastic group of people and we want you to join us!

We invite you to collaborate with us anytime by dropping us an email or by sharing some code.

Program Coordinator

We're currently accepting applications for a Program Coordinator who will help keep us organized, efficient and responsive to the needs of our communities.

Our three major projects - the Caselaw Access Project, Perma.cc and H2O all have grown significantly in adoption and complexity in recent years. We're looking to add a new LIL team member who can coordinate all of the essential and recurring activities that make those projects successful, as well as the administrative operations that keep LIL running smoothly.

As a Program Coordinator, you'll help us expand the impact of our work to meet the needs of growing communities of scholars, students, teachers, lawyers, librarians, judges, journalists and beyond. You'll also lead our continuous efforts to streamline and improve administrative process and to build productive relationships with our colleagues across Harvard.

The ideal Program Coordinator will be curious, organized, self-motivated and eager to help us refine the operational activities of the Lab. We welcome candidates with backgrounds in law, technology or librarianship, but expertise in those areas is not required.

To learn more about the position and how to apply, please click below or visit the official job posting on the Harvard Careers website.

Diversity

The work and well-being of the Lab are strengthened profoundly by the diversity of our network and our differences in background, culture, experience, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, and much more. We actively seek and welcome applications from people of color, women, the LGBTQ+ community, and persons with disabilities, as well as applications from researchers and practitioners from across the spectrum of disciplines and methods.
